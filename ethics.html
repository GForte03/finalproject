Ethical and legal issues in web development

Thinking about licensing, privacy, and inclusive design.

Copyright & licensing

Whenever I utilize code, graphics, or symbols from another source, I make sure to verify the licensing and provide credit as needed. I use original work or anything that has a clear permission for usage (like Creative Commons) when I'm not sure..

Privacy & data

Only the information you truly need should be collected by forms. If a website keeps personal information, it should describe what it gathers, how it uses it, and how users can ask for it to be deleted.

Accessibility is part of ethics (and often the law)

Making a website accessible lowers the possibility of excluding individuals with impairments and encourages more people to use it. Little actions like using labels, keyboard-friendly navigation, and semantic HTML add up.
