HTML semantic elements and structure

Building webpages with meaningful, accessible structure.

Why semantic HTML matters

Semantic elements (such as <header>, <nav>, <main>, <section>, and <footer>) assist human, screen reader, and search engine understanding of pages.

Because each section of the site has a distinct purpose, I found that applying semantic organization helped my projects feel cleaner and simpler to manage.
